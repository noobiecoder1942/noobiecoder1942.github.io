Education

Currently, I'm pursuing a master's degree in data science from Indiana University, Bloomington. Most of my coursework is centered around machine learning and statistics.

I hold a post graduate diploma in data science from International Institute of Information Technology, Bangalore. The 11-month, online, rigorous program included courses in data management, statistics and exploratory data analysis, predictive analytics, big data analytics and a capstone project.

I also hold a bachelor's degree in computer science and engineering from GCOE, Amravati.

Work Experience

Right after I graduated from GCOEA, I joined AlgoAnalytics, a data science startup in Pune, India. In my short tenure of 13 months, I got various opportunities to work on multiple client projects - courtesy of the amazing mentors I had.

One of my most challenging projects at AlgoAnalytics was implementing a research paper - Adversarially Tuned Scene Generation. It involved generating synthetic scenes using Blender, which would act as the generator for the GAN, and then eventually show that by adversarially tuning the parameters (matching pixel intensity distributions of targets and generated images) to generate more realistic images augments the performance of object detection algorithms.
